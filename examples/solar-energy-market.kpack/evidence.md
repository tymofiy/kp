# Evidence — Solar Energy Market

## E001
> **type:** multi_source_synthesis | **captured:** 2026-03-01
> **source:** Internal analysis — 10 independent assessments across multiple AI models

Multi-model synthesis across 10 analyses and 8 distinct models. All converged
on cost decline being structural (manufacturing learning curve) rather than
cyclical (subsidy-driven).

## E002
> **type:** research | **captured:** 2026-03-01
> **source:** IRENA Renewable Power Generation Costs 2025

Comprehensive dataset on utility-scale solar PV LCOE trends across 40 countries.
Learning rate of 22% per capacity doubling confirmed across 1976-2025 data.

## E003
> **type:** research | **captured:** 2026-02-14
> **source:** MIT Energy Initiative — The Future of Solar Energy (updated 2025)

Analysis of silicon cost floors and theoretical efficiency limits. Argues that
polysilicon pricing has limited room for further reduction, implying LCOE
declines will increasingly depend on balance-of-system and soft costs.

## E012
> **type:** research | **captured:** 2026-03-10
> **source:** Nature Energy — "Stability challenges for perovskite photovoltaics" (2025)

Review of degradation mechanisms: moisture ingress, thermal cycling, UV exposure.
Encapsulation advances extend lab lifetime to 5,000+ hours but field validation
at 25-year scale is absent.

## E013
> **type:** research | **captured:** 2026-03-10
> **source:** NREL Perovskite Stability Assessment 2025

Accelerated aging tests on 12 encapsulation methods. Best-performing samples
retained >90% efficiency at simulated 10-year equivalent. Gap to 25-year
warranty requirement remains the commercialization gate.

## E030
> **type:** research | **captured:** 2026-03-01
> **source:** BloombergNEF — Solar Supply Chain Vulnerability Index 2026

Quantitative assessment of geographic concentration in polysilicon, wafer,
cell, and module production. China's share exceeds 80% at every stage
except module assembly (65%). No diversification trend visible in 2025 data.
